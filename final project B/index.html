Balance

Welcome to Balance! In this game you need to help our main character get its coin! Please enjoy it and keep jumping and moving!

 About

Balance was a two-week-long project I made with the help of my professor. Moon and my classmates. As the project for my final, I aimed to let players keep the balance between different colors. Sometimes, these blocks with different colors can also be interpreted as multiple tasks in our lives. When you reach out to another, you may fail in the current one. To keep the balance, players should focus on the position of our final goals and find a better way out.

 Control

• Use the right arrow to move right

• Use the left arrow to move left

• Click the "w" to jump
